Why Thele wala

Inventory tracking

Now you do not need to maintain a register torecord your inventory. Thele waly will not only keep a record of your products, it will also issue alerts when you are low on inventory.

Khata register
Thele waly maintains your customer and vendor credit management. By sending auto generated text messages, Thele waly keeps your customers informed about what they owe you and lets them pay you back online.

Multiple delivery options

To complete the process of online selling, Thele waly offers in-app delivery services for your web store. Just a click away, Bykea and others will give you logistic support for getting your products to your customer’s door.

Accounting

Thele waly simplifies accounting for you and maintains basic records for how your business is performing. It will not only keep track of your business expenses and revenues, but will also generate sales reports for you to easily analyse your profitability.

Online payments

Point of sale (POS)
